    About Me

    Heya! 👋 I'm Andy Setiawan, an Electrical Engineering student at UBC with a passion for building robots, automation, and fixing things others gave up on. From Bali to Vancouver, my journey's been a mix of hands-on repair, tinkering with microcontrollers, and guiding others through the magic of electronics.

    I'm all about mixing creativity with logic, and I enjoy sharing what I learn along the way—whether it's making a portable oscilloscope or cracking tricky math problems. Always up for a challenge and a good chat about tech, so feel free to reach out!
